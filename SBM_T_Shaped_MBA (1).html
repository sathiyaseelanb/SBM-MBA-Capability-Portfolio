The Horizontal Bar — Breadth
Cross-Functional Fluency Across All 8 Domains
Every SBM MBA graduate develops working fluency across all eight capability domains — from Business Foundations and Data Analytics to Sustainability and Research Aptitude. These competencies allow graduates to collaborate across functions, speak every team's language, and contribute from Day 1 regardless of where they sit.

The horizontal bar is identical for every specialisation. What varies is which column the stem drops through — signalling where depth has been deliberately cultivated.
The Vertical Stem — Depth
Specialisation-Grade Expertise That Defines Professional Identity
The stem carries competencies that go beyond the general portfolio — they are specific to the student's MBA specialisation, assessed at Proficient or Exemplary band, and evidenced by multiple PARQ artifacts. Each competency includes the tools and methods the graduate can deploy, sub-skills they have developed, and an evidence-depth indicator.

Depth gives a recruiter a reason to hire this graduate specifically. Breadth gives confidence they will work across the whole organisation.
"Breadth earns you a seat at the table. Depth earns you the right to speak first."
