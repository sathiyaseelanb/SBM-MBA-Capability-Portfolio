Portfolio Design Rationale & Assessment Guidance

Each capability cluster is sequenced from foundational (knowledge-level) to applied (practice-level) competencies, reflecting a developmental arc across the MBA programme. Day-1-Readiness is defined as the demonstrable ability to contribute independently within an organisational context from the first week of professional placement — not merely knowledge possession, but situated, transferable performance.

Portfolio evidence should be assessed against four proficiency bands. Faculty assessors and corporate mentors are encouraged to use the rubric descriptors for each band when evaluating capstone projects, internship reports, and placement-readiness interviews.
