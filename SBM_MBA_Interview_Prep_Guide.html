MBA Interview Preparation Guide

Domain-wise · STAR Builder · Assessment Point–aligned · Common MBA + Specialisation

How to use this guide

This guide is aligned to your MBA Capability Self-Assessment. Your Assessment Point determines which domain interview questions are unlocked — questions are introduced progressively as you complete each trimester and build the relevant capability.

For each question: read what the recruiter is testing, then use the STAR builder to draft your answer using real evidence from your portfolio. Your answer to RBT-3 Apply level questions should always include a specific, named situation — not a general claim.

Generic questions are always accessible — begin there before moving to domain-specific preparation.

What the recruiter is testingThe recruiter wants a 90-second professional narrative — not a CV recitation. They are testing whether you know what is relevant, can organise your story around themes rather than chronology, and can land on why you are here for this role specifically.

What the recruiter is testingThe recruiter is testing whether you have a repeatable diagnostic method. They want to see structure before solution — not a good outcome that arrived accidentally.
